Welcome to the Official Internal Affairs (IA) Handbook for Oklahoma RP. This document outlines the protocols, responsibilities, and standards expected of Internal Affairs staff and those subject to review. It promotes transparency, fairness, and accountability within our department.

Mission Statement

The mission of the Internal Affairs Division is to uphold integrity, transparency, and fairness in all operations. IA ensures all personnel meet the highest standards of professionalism and conduct.

Roles and Responsibilities

IA Agent Responsibilities

  • Impartially investigate allegations of misconduct
  • Preserve confidentiality and neutrality
  • Maintain comprehensive and timely records
  • Monitor compliance across departments

IA Supervisor Responsibilities

  • Oversee and assign investigations
  • Evaluate agent reports and findings
  • Recommend corrective or disciplinary actions

Investigation Process

  1. Receive and log complaint
  2. Assign to an IA agent
  3. Collect and review all relevant evidence
  4. Conduct interviews and compile findings
  5. Supervisor reviews and makes recommendations
  6. Case outcome documented and archived

Reporting a Complaint

Complaints should be submitted through our official ticket system with supporting evidence. All reports are handled confidentially and with care. Once received, you will receive updates throughout the process.

Types of Misconduct

  • Abuse of power or authority
  • Harassment, discrimination, or bias
  • Corruption or unethical actions
  • Neglect of duty or violation of procedures

Disciplinary Actions

  • Verbal Warning: Initial corrective feedback
  • Written Reprimand: Documented warning in personnel file
  • Training/Retraining: Reeducation on policy or behavior
  • Suspension: Temporary removal from duty
  • Demotion: Lowering of rank/responsibilities
  • Termination: Dismissal from the department
