Mission Statement

The Internal Affairs Division upholds integrity, fairness, and accountability throughout Oklahoma RP. We investigate reports thoroughly to ensure transparency and justice within the ranks.

Roles and Responsibilities

IA Agents

  • Conduct investigations
  • Maintain logs
  • Ensure fair process

IA Supervisors

  • Oversee agents
  • Assign cases
  • Submit final recommendations

Investigation Process

  1. Ticket submitted
  2. Agent assigned
  3. Evidence gathered
  4. All parties interviewed
  5. Decision made within 72 hours

Disciplinary Actions

  • Verbal Warning
  • Written Warning
  • Suspension
  • Demotion
  • Termination
