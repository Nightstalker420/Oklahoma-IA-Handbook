Mission Statement

The Internal Affairs Division upholds integrity by investigating allegations of misconduct, enforcing department standards, and preserving trust within Oklahoma RP.

Roles and Responsibilities

IA Agent

  • Investigate complaints fairly and thoroughly
  • Ensure neutrality and confidentiality
  • Collect and analyze evidence
  • Submit detailed reports to supervisors

IA Supervisor

  • Oversee investigation processes
  • Review and validate agent reports
  • Determine and assign disciplinary actions

Investigation Process

  1. Complaint submitted via ticket system or form
  2. Assigned to an available IA Agent
  3. Evidence and witness statements collected
  4. Agent interviews involved parties
  5. Findings written and submitted to Supervisor
  6. Supervisor reviews and issues a ruling
  7. Case is logged and archived

Step-by-Step: Handling a Deputy Report Ticket

  1. Claim the ticket and introduce yourself.
  2. Ask for:
    • Incident summary
    • Date/time
    • Evidence (screenshots/clips)
    • Usernames/badge numbers
  3. Ask clarifying questions.
  4. Interview mentioned parties privately.
  5. Log findings in the IA Case Log.
  6. Submit findings to IA Supervisor.

How to Use the PAWA Bot for Recording

Use /record in a channel to start a recording in an IA voice chat. PAWA bot helps document interviews and formal discussions.

  1. Join the IA VC.
  2. Type /record.
  3. PAWA will join and begin recording.
  4. Say clearly the session is recorded and all parties agree.
  5. To stop, type /record stop.

Misuse will result in disciplinary action.

Reporting a Complaint

File reports using tickets or the IA Complaint Form. Include all evidence. IA staff will keep it confidential and provide updates.

Types of Misconduct

  • Abuse of power
  • Harassment or discrimination
  • Corruption or unethical actions
  • Negligence of duties

Disciplinary Actions

  • Verbal Warning: Informal coaching
  • Written Reprimand: Logged warning
  • Retraining: Policy re-education
  • Suspension: Temp removal from duties
  • Demotion: Rank reduction
  • Termination: Full removal from department

Zero Tolerance Policy

Strictly enforced for:

  • Misuse of IA powers
  • Harassment or retaliation
  • Evidence tampering or leaking
  • Corruption or unauthorized actions
