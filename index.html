Follow these steps to properly log a case using the official IA Case Submission Form.

  1. Access the Google Form: Use the link provided below.
  2. Complete All Fields:
    • Officer Under Investigation
    • Investigating Staff Member
    • Date of Incident
    • Case Type
    • Incident Description
    • Upload Evidence (screenshots, clips, logs)
    • Case Outcome
    • Internal Notes (optional)
  3. Submit: Double-check your entries, then hit Submit to log your case.

Reminders

  • Keep it confidential: Do not share reports outside of IA.
  • Be detailed: Clear reports speed up investigations.
  • Reach out: Contact IA Command if you need help submitting.
