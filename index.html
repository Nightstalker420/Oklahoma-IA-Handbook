Oklahoma RP Internal Affairs Handbook

Welcome to the Official Internal Affairs (IA) Handbook for Oklahoma RP. This document outlines the policies, procedures, responsibilities, and standards required of Internal Affairs personnel and those subject to IA oversight.

Mission Statement

The mission of the Internal Affairs Division is to uphold the values of honesty, fairness, and integrity in all departmental operations.

Roles and Responsibilities

IA Agent Responsibilities

  • Investigate allegations of misconduct
  • Remain impartial and professional
  • Maintain confidentiality
  • Submit timely reports
  • Monitor department conduct

IA Supervisor Responsibilities

  • Assign and oversee investigations
  • Review final findings
  • Recommend disciplinary actions

Investigation Process

  1. Complaint received and logged
  2. IA agent assigned
  3. Evidence collected
  4. Report compiled
  5. Supervisor review
  6. Case closed and archived

Reporting a Complaint

Submit a ticket with evidence and a detailed description. Confidentiality is required throughout the process.

Types of Misconduct

  • Abuse of authority
  • Discrimination or harassment
  • Corruption
  • Failure to follow protocols

Disciplinary Actions

  • Verbal Warning
  • Written Reprimand
  • Training or Retraining
  • Suspension
  • Demotion
  • Termination
