🛡️ Internal Affairs Case Submission

Instruction Sheet – Oklahoma RP IA

📋 How to Submit a Case Report

  1. Open the Case Submission Form
    You will receive a Google Form link from the IA Overseer or Assistant.
    Keep this link private and do not share it with non-IA members.
  2. Fill Out the Required Fields
    Complete all parts of the form:
    • Officer Under Investigation – Roblox or Discord name.
    • Investigating Staff Member – Your name as the IA investigator.
    • Date of Incident – The date the incident occurred.
    • Case Type – Choose from: Misconduct, Abuse of Power, etc.
    • Incident Description – A full and detailed report.
    • Upload Evidence – Screenshots, videos, chat logs (max 10MB).
    • Case Outcome – Current status of the case (Pending, Warning, etc.).
    • Internal Notes / Comments – Any extra notes about the case (optional).
  3. Submit the Form
    Review your answers and click “Submit” at the bottom. The case will be logged into the department’s secure case tracker.

📌 Reminders & Guidelines

  • Confidentiality is mandatory. Do not share case info outside IA.
  • Submit cases promptly after incidents are reported.
  • Clearly label all uploaded evidence (e.g., Screenshot_Abuse_6-29.png).
  • For urgent cases, notify a Supervisor or Overseer immediately after submission.

❓ Need Help?

If you have trouble submitting or accessing the form, contact your IA Trainer, Assistant Overseer, or Department Overseer.
